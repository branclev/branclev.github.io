Neon Drift — Delete Your Data

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Neon Drift (developed by branclev) doesn't use traditional accounts — there's no email, password, or login. Instead, each player has a self-chosen callsign that identifies their entries on the leaderboards. This page explains how to have that data deleted.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to YOUR_EMAIL_HERE with the subject line "Delete my data".
  2. Include the exact callsign you used in the game.
  3. We'll remove your leaderboard entries (All-Time, Daily, and Storm) associated with that callsign, typically within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your callsign and all associated leaderboard scores (All-Time, Daily, and Storm)
  • Your recorded level and equipped skin shown alongside those scores

What isn't affected

  • Local device data — your in-game progress, unlocked cosmetics, and settings are stored only on your own device and are not something we can remotely delete. You can clear this yourself at any time by clearing the app's storage/data through your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.
  • Backups — data may briefly persist in routine database backups after a deletion request, until those backups naturally cycle out. It is removed from all active systems immediately upon request.

For more detail on what we collect and why, see our Privacy Policy.
