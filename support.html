Contact

Email us at Branclevy@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. Please include your in-game callsign and, if it's about a purchase, the approximate date.

Common questions

  • I got a new device — where are my purchases and progress? Sign in with the same account you linked before and tap Restore Purchases in the Store. Your callsign, leaderboard history, and purchased content come back.
  • How do the daily challenges work? Everyone flies the same seeded course each day. You get one run at the global daily board; the challenge resets at midnight (UTC).
  • How do I unlock skins? Most are earned through scores, survival streaks, podium finishes, and multiplayer wins. Some are available as optional cosmetic purchases.
  • How do I delete my data? If your callsign is linked to an account, open Settings → Profile → Danger Zone → Delete My Data. Full details are on our data deletion page.
