Neon Drift — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Neon Drift ("the game," "we," "us") is a single-player arcade game with optional online leaderboards. This page explains what information the game collects, how it's used, and what choices you have.

Information we collect

When you set a callsign (username) and play, the game may collect and store:

  • Your chosen callsign — a name you pick yourself. It doesn't need to be, and shouldn't be, your real name.
  • Game scores and stats — your scores, level, and equipped cosmetic skin, used to display leaderboards (All-Time, Daily, and Storm).
  • Local game progress — settings, unlocked cosmetics, and progress are stored locally on your device (via browser local storage) and are not tied to any personal identity.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, or photos. We do not use advertising identifiers, and the game currently contains no ads.

How your information is used

Callsigns, scores, and related stats are used solely to operate the game's leaderboard features — showing rankings, daily challenge results, and podium history. This data is not used for advertising, profiling, or sold to third parties.

Where your information is stored

Leaderboard data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database hosting provider. Local game settings and progress are stored only on your own device and are never transmitted except when directly relevant to a leaderboard submission (score, level, equipped skin) or, if you choose to link your account, account linking (see below).

Data retention

All-time and Storm leaderboard entries are retained indefinitely, tied to your chosen callsign, so your best score can be displayed and updated over time. Daily Challenge leaderboard entries older than 60 days are automatically deleted on a routine basis.

Account linking

Neon Drift offers an optional feature that lets you link your chosen callsign to your Google account using Google Sign-In. This is intended to:

  • Help prevent other players from using a callsign that isn't theirs, and
  • Let your callsign and leaderboard history be restored if you switch devices, reinstall the game, or clear your browser data.

This feature is optional — the game continues to work with a self-chosen callsign alone if you prefer not to link an account. If you choose to link your account, we store a unique identifier provided by Google Sign-In, associated with your callsign, solely to verify ownership and restore your leaderboard history. We do not access your Google account's real name, email address, contacts, or other Google account data beyond what's needed for this verification.

If you rename a linked callsign, your leaderboard history (All-Time, Daily, and Storm) is moved to the new name as part of that process.

Your choices

If you haven't linked a Google account (see above), your callsign is self-chosen and not tied to any verified identity, so we're unable to confirm ownership of a specific callsign on our own. If you'd like a leaderboard entry removed, or need help with a linked account, contact us using the information below and we'll do our best to assist.

Children's privacy

Neon Drift is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time as the game evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
