Neon Drift — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Neon Drift ("the game," "we," "us") is an arcade game with optional online leaderboards, social features, and cosmetic purchases. This page explains what information the game collects, how it's used, and what choices you have.

Information we collect

When you set a callsign (username) and play, the game may collect and store:

  • Your chosen callsign — a name you pick yourself. It doesn't need to be, and shouldn't be, your real name.
  • Game scores and stats — your scores, level, and equipped cosmetic skin, used to display leaderboards (All-Time, Daily, and Storm) and multiplayer results.
  • Multiplayer match data — if you play Versus matches or Last Pilot Standing, the results of those matches (participating callsigns, scores, outcomes, and equipped skins) are stored as match history and win records.
  • Local game progress — settings, unlocked cosmetics, and progress are stored locally on your device (via browser local storage). If you link a Google account (see below), your experience points and purchase-based unlocks are also backed up to our servers so they can be restored on other devices.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, or photos. We do not use advertising identifiers, and the game currently contains no ads.

How your information is used

Callsigns, scores, and related stats are used solely to operate the game's features — showing rankings, daily challenge results, podium history, multiplayer matches, and restoring your progress and purchases across devices. This data is not used for advertising or profiling, and is not sold to third parties.

Where your information is stored

Online game data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database hosting provider. Local game settings and progress are stored only on your own device and are never transmitted except when directly relevant to a game feature (leaderboard submissions, multiplayer matches, account linking, purchases, or progress backup as described on this page).

Purchases

Neon Drift offers optional cosmetic purchases (individual skins and a Supporter Pack). All payments are processed entirely by Google Play — we never receive or store your payment card details, billing address, or other payment information. To deliver what you bought and keep it restorable on your other devices, we store a record of each purchase (the product purchased and a purchase token issued by Google Play) associated with your linked Google account identifier. We verify purchases and check for refunds directly with Google Play; if a purchase is refunded, the associated content is removed from your account.

Purchases require a linked Google account (see below) so they can be restored if you switch devices or reinstall the game.

Data retention

All-time and Storm leaderboard entries are retained indefinitely, tied to your chosen callsign, so your best score can be displayed and updated over time. Daily Challenge leaderboard entries older than 60 days are automatically deleted on a routine basis. Multiplayer match history and win records are retained as point-in-time records. Purchase records are retained while your entitlement to the purchased content remains active.

Account linking

Neon Drift offers an optional feature that lets you link your chosen callsign to your Google account using Google Sign-In. This is intended to:

  • Help prevent other players from using a callsign that isn't theirs,
  • Let your callsign, leaderboard history, progress, and purchases be restored if you switch devices, reinstall the game, or clear your browser data, and
  • Enable social features (Friends, Versus challenges, Last Pilot Standing lobbies, and purchases), which are only available to linked accounts.

This feature is optional — the game continues to work with a self-chosen callsign alone if you prefer not to link an account, though social features and purchases won't be available. If you choose to link your account, we store a unique identifier provided by Google Sign-In, associated with your callsign. We do not access your Google account's real name, email address, contacts, or other Google account data beyond what's needed for this verification.

If you rename a linked callsign, your leaderboard history (All-Time, Daily, and Storm) is moved to the new name as part of that process.

Friends

If you use the optional Friends feature, we store the friend relationships between linked accounts (requests sent, accepted, and declined) so friends lists, friend leaderboards, and friend-only game modes work. Removing a friend deletes that relationship record.

Push notifications

If you choose to enable push notifications (for friend requests, challenge invitations, and match results), we store a device push token issued by Firebase Cloud Messaging, associated with your linked account. This token identifies your device for message delivery only. It is deleted when you turn notifications off in the game's settings, and tokens that permanently stop working (for example, after an uninstall) are removed automatically.

Your choices

You can play without linking any account. You can turn push notifications off at any time in Settings. If you'd like your data removed, see our data deletion page, or contact us using the information below. If you haven't linked a Google account, your callsign is self-chosen and not tied to any verified identity, so we may be unable to confirm ownership of a specific callsign, but we'll do our best to assist.

Children's privacy

Neon Drift is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time as the game evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
